How I work with clients.

Discovery

I start by listening. Tell me about your project, timeline, budget, and what success looks like. No commitment required for initial conversations.

Planning

Clear roadmap

Once we align, I create a structured plan with milestones, deliverables, and transparent pricing. You'll know exactly what to expect at each stage.

Execution

Regular updates

I work in sprints with regular check-ins. You'll see progress continuously, not just at the end. Feedback loops are built into the process.

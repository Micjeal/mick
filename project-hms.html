Healthcare case study

Hospital Management System

Multi-role dashboards, inventory management, and blood bank tracking for hospital operations.

System focus.

The goal was to organize hospital workflows into clear role-based interfaces with practical data handling and fast access to operational records.

  • React.js and Tailwind CSS interface
  • Node.js and MySQL backend direction
  • Role-based dashboard patterns
  • Inventory and blood bank workflows

Highlights.

Access

Role-based dashboards

Separate views for admins, clinical staff, and operations keep each workflow focused and reduce accidental exposure of sensitive records.

Operations

Inventory & blood bank

Stock levels and blood units are tracked in one place, with fast access to the records that day-to-day hospital work depends on.

Clarity

Built around real tasks

Screens are organized around the jobs people actually do, so the system stays legible, quick to navigate, and easy to audit.

About

Practical engineering for products people can actually use.

I am a software engineer based in Kampala, Uganda, focused on secure systems, product interfaces, AI features, and maintainable full-stack builds.

Where my work sits.

Education

Software engineering foundation

Diploma in Software Engineering at ISBAT University, with a final year project focused on secure banking workflows. Graduated with distinction in system architecture and security.

Build style

Clean systems over noisy features

I care about clear interfaces, role-based flows, practical data structures, and code that is easy to improve. Every line I write considers the next developer who will read it.

Focus

Web, mobile, and AI products

Portfolio sites, dashboards, admin tools, MVPs, AI-assisted features, and system redesigns. I particularly enjoy solving problems that sit between user experience and technical architecture.

My journey.

Started

Curiosity-driven learning

Growth

Real-world projects

From student assignments to production systems for healthcare, finance, and operations. Each project taught me something new about security, performance, and user needs.

Today

Building with purpose

Now focused on creating software that actually solves problems. I choose projects that challenge me and have meaningful impact on the people who use them.
